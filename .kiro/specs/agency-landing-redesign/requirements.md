# Requirements Document

## Introduction

Transform the existing personal portfolio website into a professional agency landing page for Make Release, a tailored software solutions digital agency. The redesign will maintain the existing technical foundation while completely restructuring the content, messaging, and visual identity to represent an agency rather than an individual developer.

## Glossary

- **Make_Release_Website**: The redesigned agency landing page system
- **Agency_Services**: The software development services offered by Make Release
- **Client_Portfolio**: Collection of agency work examples and case studies
- **Contact_System**: Lead generation and client inquiry management system
- **Hero_Section**: The primary above-the-fold content area
- **Service_Showcase**: Section displaying agency capabilities and offerings

## Requirements

### Requirement 1

**User Story:** As a potential client, I want to immediately understand what Make Release offers, so that I can quickly determine if they can help with my software needs

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. WHEN a visitor loads the homepage, THE Make_Release_Website SHALL display a clear value proposition within the Hero_Section
2. THE Make_Release_Website SHALL present the agency name "Make Release" prominently in the header
3. THE Make_Release_Website SHALL include a tagline that communicates "tailored software solutions" messaging
4. THE Make_Release_Website SHALL display primary service categories within the first viewport
5. WHEN a visitor views the Hero_Section, THE Make_Release_Website SHALL provide a clear call-to-action button for engagement

### Requirement 2

**User Story:** As a business owner, I want to see examples of Make Release's work, so that I can evaluate their capabilities and expertise

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE Make_Release_Website SHALL display a Client_Portfolio section with at least 4 project examples
2. WHEN a visitor clicks on a portfolio item, THE Make_Release_Website SHALL show detailed project information including technologies used and business outcomes
3. THE Make_Release_Website SHALL include client testimonials or case study highlights
4. THE Make_Release_Website SHALL organize portfolio items by service type or industry
5. WHEN displaying portfolio items, THE Make_Release_Website SHALL include visual previews of the completed work

### Requirement 3

**User Story:** As a decision maker, I want to understand Make Release's service offerings, so that I can determine if they match my project requirements

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE Make_Release_Website SHALL display a Service_Showcase section with detailed service descriptions
2. THE Make_Release_Website SHALL list core services including web development, mobile applications, and custom software solutions
3. WHEN a visitor views service information, THE Make_Release_Website SHALL include technology stacks and methodologies used
4. THE Make_Release_Website SHALL present service benefits and outcomes for each offering
5. THE Make_Release_Website SHALL include pricing approach or engagement model information

### Requirement 4

**User Story:** As a prospective client, I want to easily contact Make Release, so that I can discuss my project requirements

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE Make_Release_Website SHALL provide a Contact_System with multiple communication options
2. WHEN a visitor submits a contact form, THE Make_Release_Website SHALL collect project requirements and contact information
3. THE Make_Release_Website SHALL display business contact information including email and phone
4. THE Make_Release_Website SHALL include a project inquiry form with relevant fields for software projects
5. THE Make_Release_Website SHALL provide estimated response time for inquiries

### Requirement 5

**User Story:** As a mobile user, I want the agency website to work seamlessly on my device, so that I can explore services and contact the agency from anywhere

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE Make_Release_Website SHALL maintain responsive design across all device sizes
2. WHEN accessed on mobile devices, THE Make_Release_Website SHALL provide touch-friendly navigation
3. THE Make_Release_Website SHALL ensure all content sections are accessible on mobile devices
4. THE Make_Release_Website SHALL maintain fast loading times on mobile connections
5. WHEN viewing portfolio items on mobile, THE Make_Release_Website SHALL provide optimized image display and navigation
